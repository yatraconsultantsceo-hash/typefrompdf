Refund Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026  ·  All plans covered

✓

14-day money-back guarantee: If you're not satisfied with TypeFromPDF for any reason, email us within 14 calendar days of your purchase and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

01 Our Commitment

We want you to feel completely confident purchasing a TypeFromPDF subscription. That's why every paid plan — Monthly, Yearly, and Student — comes with a 14-day money-back guarantee.

If you change your mind for any reason within 14 calendar days of completing your purchase, we will refund 100% of the amount you paid. No questions, no conditions, no hassle.

02 Eligibility

A refund may be requested if all of the following are true:

  • The refund request is submitted within 14 calendar days of the original purchase date.
  • Your subscription was purchased directly through TypeFromPDF at typefrompdf.com (via our payment processor, Paddle).
  • You have not previously received a refund for the same subscription or plan type within the last 12 months.

Refund requests made after 14 calendar days of purchase are not eligible for a refund. Exceptional circumstances (e.g., duplicate charges, billing errors by Paddle) will always be investigated and resolved.

03 How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is simple. Follow these steps:

1
Email us within 14 days
Send an email to support@typefrompdf.com with the subject line "Refund Request".
2
Include your purchase details
Please include the email address used to purchase, and optionally the Paddle order ID from your receipt email (e.g., Order #123456).
3
We process within 1 business day
We'll confirm your refund and submit it to Paddle within 1 business day. Funds typically appear in 3–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

04 Refund Processing & Timing

All payments and subscriptions are processed by Paddle (paddle.com), our payment processor. Once we approve your refund request, we initiate the refund through Paddle. The time for the credit to appear on your statement is determined by your bank or card network, typically:

  • Credit / debit cards: 3–10 business days
  • PayPal: 3–5 business days

You will receive an email confirmation from Paddle when the refund has been issued.

05 Subscription Cancellation

Requesting a refund also cancels your subscription immediately. Your plan will be downgraded to Free upon processing of the refund, and you will lose access to premium features (unlimited PDFs, all 18 programming languages, code file uploads).

To cancel your subscription without requesting a refund, you can manage your billing directly at customer.paddle.com. Cancellation stops future charges but does not trigger a refund for the current billing period.

06 Situations Not Covered

The following are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Refund requests made more than 14 calendar days after purchase.
  • Accounts that have already received a refund for the same plan within the last 12 months.
  • Subscriptions purchased through third-party platforms (if any) — please refer to that platform's own refund policy.

If you believe you have an exceptional situation (e.g., a double charge, technical error preventing access), please email us and we will always investigate fairly.

07 Contact Us

For refunds, billing questions, or any issues with your subscription:

  • Email: support@typefrompdf.com
  • Contact form: typefrompdf.com/contact

We respond to all refund requests within 1 business day (usually much faster).
