How It Works

Simple 3-step process.

From posting your video to getting Premium access — here's exactly how it works.

1
Create & publish your video
Make an original video showcasing TypeFromPDF and publish it on TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Facebook Reels, or X.
2
Reach a milestone
Once your video hits 2,000+ views (or any higher milestone), you're eligible to claim your reward.
3
Email us your video link
Send the link to your post to support@typefrompdf.com with the subject line "Creator Rewards Submission". We'll reply with a short verification form.
4
Use the same email address
Sign in to your TypeFromPDF account using the same email address you submit on the verification form. This is how we credit the right account.
5
We verify and activate your reward
Our team reviews your submission — video, platform, and view count. Once approved, your free Premium subscription is added to your account automatically.

What We Verify

  • The original video link and the platform it was published on
  • The current view count at the time of submission
  • That the content is original and created by you
  • That the content follows our community guidelines
  • That your TypeFromPDF account email matches the verification form email

Important Notes

  • Videos must be original and created by you.
  • Fake views, bots, or artificial engagement are not allowed and will result in disqualification.
  • Rewards are granted only after successful manual verification by our team.
  • We reserve the right to reject submissions that don't meet our guidelines.
  • Only one reward is granted per qualifying video unless otherwise approved.
  • If your video reaches a higher tier after your initial claim, contact us to upgrade your reward.
